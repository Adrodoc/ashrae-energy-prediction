EnergApp is the phase 2 of our Kaggle Challange. The aim of EnergApp is to incorporate the predicted model from phase 1. The idea behind this is that users can submit their building's data and in return they get the predicted energy consumption for said building.

In order to submit and manage your buildings, you have to sign up for our web application.

You can add your buildings by going to Buildings and than clicking on the add button.

You can manage your buildings by going to Buildings. There you will get a list of all your submitted buildings.

We trained our model by using Light GBM, which is a high-performance gradient boosting framework based on decision tree algorithm (GBDT).

Procedure

We provide our model in txt-format, which can be downloaded from here and save it under save it as ./app/model/.

Our app comes with a built in API to receive energy predictions. To get a prediction you need to attach a json payload containing various attributes. We provide a list of all available attributes as well as an example request can be found in our GitHub repository: https://github.com/energeeks/ashrae-energy-prediction.

You can check us out in GitHub at https://github.com/energeeks/ashrae-energy-prediction. From there you can contact the contributors of this project.
